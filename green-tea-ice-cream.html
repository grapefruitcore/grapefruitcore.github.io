Green Tea Ice Cream

Requires: ice cream machine. Makes 1 pint - fits one Ninja Creami container. Quadruple this recipe for a standard 2 liter ice cream machine.

Ingredients

  • 450 mL half and half cream
  • 1/4 cup loose leaf green tea
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 3 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp corn syrup
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

Steeping the tea

  1. Reserve half of the cream.
  2. Heat the rest of the cream in a saucepan to boiling, then add tea leaves.
  3. Reduce heat to a simmer and allow to steep for 30 min.
  4. Strain leaves from steeped tea cream mixture and place the liquid in the fridge to cool.

Custard base

  1. Whisk the reserved cream with egg yolks, sugar, and corn syrup.
  2. Heat the custard in a saucepan, stirring occasionally, until a temperature of 75 C is reached. Maintain temperature for at least 5 minutes.
  3. Remove from heat and add vanilla extract.

Churning

  1. When the custard base is no longer hot, mix the steeped tea cream and the custard base.
  2. If using a Ninja Creami, freeze for 16-24hr and put it in on the "ice cream" setting. In a standard ice cream maker, churn for 1 hour.
