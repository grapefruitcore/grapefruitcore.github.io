Shaved ice cold noodles

Requires: Slow cooker or large pot, Ninja Creami or blender. Makes 4 servings.

Ingredients

Shaved ice broth

Can be made in advance and stored in the freezer!

Note: if substituting for storebought broth and using Ninja Creami, you must add at least 1 packet gelatin per 1 litre of broth or you will break your machine.

  • 350g chicken wing tips
  • 1 litre water
  • 1 slice ginger
  • 2 green onions
  • 2 tsp salt

Noodles and toppings

  • 4 bundles dried soba noodles
  • 1 cucumber
  • 4 boiled eggs (try it with soy marinated eggs for extra flavour)
  • Any other desired cold toppings
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp gochujang
  • 1 tbsp aged rice vinegar
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • Mint leaves for garnish (optional)

Instructions

Shaved ice broth

  1. Sear chicken wing tips until skin has a light crisp.
  2. Add broth ingredients to slow cooker or large pot.
  3. Slow cook/simmer on low for 16-24 hours.
  4. Strain out solids.
  5. Measure one pint of the broth per Ninja Creami container, if using, and freeze. If using blender, pour broth into ice cube tray and freeze.
  6. In Ninja Creami, use setting 'Sorbet'. In blender, blend until desired texture is reached.

Noodles and toppings

  1. Boil noodles according to directions for serving cold.
  2. Julienne cucumber and slice boiled eggs in half.
  3. Combine liquid ingredients. Add noodles and mix.
  4. Divide noodles evenly amongst 4 bowls.
  5. Divide shaved ice broth evenly amongst 4 bowls and add desired toppings.
